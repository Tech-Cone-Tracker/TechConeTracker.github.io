Future Tech Predictions

Improvement of AI

  • Models like ChatGPT and others will become larger and more capable, leading to even more general-purpose AI systems that can understand and perform a wider array of tasks.
  • Use of AI will become more and more commonplace, similar to how smartphones and the internet became commonplace.
  • People may become overly dependent on AI.

Improvement of Quantum Computing

  • The ability of quantum computing to be used for cryptography and complex problem-solving will be increased.

Improvement of Virtual and Digital Worlds

  • Metaverse environments will become more common and accepted as real places to communicate for work, socializing, and entertainment.

Improvement of Medicine

  • Personalized medicines using genomics and AI.
  • Neural implants to cure diseases like Parkinson's or Alzheimer's.
